# Caption Strategy — Expert Knowledge Base

## Caption Formulas That Drive Engagement

### The Hook + Value + CTA Formula
Best general-purpose formula. Works across all platforms.
```
[Hook line that stops the scroll]

[2-4 sentences of value, insight, or story]

[CTA — question, instruction, or prompt]
```

Example:
> Stop posting at "the best time." Here's what actually matters.
>
> The algorithm doesn't care what time you post. It cares how your first 50-100 viewers respond. If your content gets saves and shares in the first 30 minutes, it gets pushed to more people — regardless of whether you posted at 9am or 2am.
>
> What's the post you're most proud of that flopped? Drop it below 👇

### The List Formula
Highly saveable content. Works especially well on Instagram.
```
[X] [things/tips/mistakes/reasons] that [outcome]:

1. [Item] — [brief explanation]
2. [Item] — [brief explanation]
3. [Item] — [brief explanation]

Save this for later 🔖
```

### The Story Formula
Best for building personal brand and trust.
```
[Dramatic opening line]

[Set the scene — 1-2 sentences]
[The conflict or challenge]
[The turning point]
[The result or lesson]

[Takeaway the audience can apply]
```

### The Contrarian / Hot Take Formula
Drives comments (including disagreement, which the algorithm loves).
```
[Unpopular opinion / hot take]

[Why most people get this wrong]
[Your evidence or experience]
[What to do instead]

Agree or disagree? Tell me why 👇
```

### The Question-Led Formula
Drives comments directly.
```
[Relatable question or "am I the only one who..."]

[Expand on the situation]
[Optional: share your answer/perspective]

[Ask the audience their take]
```

## Hashtag Strategy Per Platform

### TikTok
- **3-5 hashtags** is the sweet spot
- Mix: 1 broad (#fyp is debatable but still used), 1-2 niche (#smallbusinesstips), 1-2 content-specific (#productreview)
- **TikTok SEO hashtags** matter more than ever — TikTok is a search engine now. Use hashtags that people actually search for.
- Check TikTok Creative Center and search bar autocomplete for trending/searchable hashtags
- Don't use 15+ hashtags — it looks spammy and doesn't improve reach
- **#fyp, #foryou, #viral** — marginal impact at best; use the character space for descriptive hashtags instead

### Instagram
- **3-5 hashtags** (Instagram officially recommends this over the old 30-hashtag strategy)
- Mix: 1 broad (500K-5M posts), 2 medium (50K-500K posts), 1-2 niche (<50K posts)
- Place hashtags **in the caption** (not comments — IG confirmed caption placement is better for discovery)
- Avoid banned/spammy hashtags (check if a hashtag has a page — if it doesn't, it might be restricted)
- **Alt text** is more valuable than hashtags for IG discovery now (see SEO section below)

### YouTube
- **Hashtags in title**: Max 3, placed at the beginning or end of the title. Shows as clickable links above the title.
- **Hashtags in description**: YouTube indexes these but they're less impactful than title keywords
- **Tags** (separate from hashtags): Add 5-10 relevant tags in the tag field. Include: exact topic, broader category, competitor names, common misspellings.
- YouTube SEO relies more on **title, description, and transcript** than hashtags/tags

## CTA Types and When to Use Them

### Engagement CTAs (Algorithm Fuel)
Use these to boost engagement metrics, which drives distribution:
- **"Save this for later 🔖"** — drives saves (IG algorithm loves saves)
- **"Share this with someone who needs it"** — drives shares (strongest signal on all platforms)
- **"Drop a 🔥 if you agree"** — low-friction comment prompt
- **"What's your experience with this? Comment below"** — drives quality comments
- **"Follow for Part 2"** — drives follows and creates anticipation

### Conversion CTAs (Business Goals)
Use these when the goal is traffic or sales:
- **"Link in bio"** — standard for IG (use a link-in-bio tool: Linktree, Stan Store, or custom)
- **"Comment [keyword] and I'll send you the link"** — drives comments AND captures leads (use ManyChat or similar automation)
- **"Shop now — link in bio"** — direct for ecommerce
- **"DM me [keyword] for details"** — drives DMs, personal touch for services/high-ticket
- **"Tap the link in our story"** — for IG when you have a story link up

### Content CTAs (Growth)
- **"Follow for more [topic]"** — simple but effective, especially for new accounts
- **"Turn on notifications 🔔"** — aggressive but works for series content
- **"What should I cover next?"** — engagement + content ideas

### CTA Placement
- **End of caption** for written CTAs
- **End of video** (last 3-5 seconds) for verbal CTAs
- **On-screen text** CTA in the final frame
- **Don't put the CTA first** — earn attention before asking for action
- **One primary CTA per post** — multiple CTAs dilute each other

## Character Limits & Formatting

### Platform Character Limits
| Platform | Caption Limit | Display Before "More" |
|---|---|---|
| TikTok | 4,000 characters | ~80-100 characters (first 2 lines) |
| Instagram | 2,200 characters | ~125 characters (first 2 lines) |
| YouTube Shorts | 100 char title + 5,000 char description | Title only in feed |
| YouTube Long-form | 100 char title + 5,000 char description | Title + first 2-3 lines of description |

### Formatting Best Practices
- **Line breaks** — use short paragraphs (1-2 sentences). Wall of text = nobody reads it.
- **Emojis as bullets** — ✅ ⚡ 🔑 → Use sparingly as visual anchors, not every line
- **First line is everything** — front-load the hook before the "...more" truncation
- **ALL CAPS sparingly** — one word or phrase for emphasis, not entire sentences
- **Spacing** — blank lines between sections improve readability on mobile
- **No links in IG captions** — they're not clickable. Use "link in bio" instead.

### TikTok Caption Strategy
- Keep it **short** (1-3 sentences) — TikTok users don't read long captions
- Use captions to **add context** the video doesn't provide
- **Controversial or question captions** drive comments: "Is it just me?" "Wait for it…" "Should I do Part 2?"
- The caption appears over the video — keep it short so it doesn't block important visuals

### Instagram Caption Strategy
- **Long-form captions** work well for educational/personal brand content (300-800 characters)
- **Short captions** for entertainment/visual content (under 100 characters)
- First line must be strong enough to make people tap "...more"
- Use **carousel posts** when you have content that deserves a longer caption — people engage more

## Hook Lines That Stop the Scroll

### Proven Hook Templates
**Curiosity hooks:**
- "Nobody talks about this but…"
- "I was today years old when I learned…"
- "The thing about [topic] that everyone gets wrong…"
- "Here's what they don't tell you about [topic]…"
- "I tested [thing] for 30 days. Here's what happened."

**Relatable hooks:**
- "POV: [relatable scenario]"
- "Tell me you're a [identity] without telling me you're a [identity]"
- "Things I wish I knew before [experience]"
- "Am I the only one who [common experience]?"

**Value hooks:**
- "Save this — you'll need it later"
- "[Number] [things] that will change your [area of life]"
- "The only [topic] guide you'll ever need"
- "Stop doing [common mistake]. Do this instead."

**Urgency/news hooks:**
- "[Platform/brand] just changed everything"
- "This won't last long"
- "Breaking: [news relevant to audience]"
- "If you're a [audience], you need to see this"

## SEO in Captions

### TikTok SEO
TikTok is now a search engine for Gen Z and millennials. Optimize:
- Include **searchable keywords** in captions (what would someone type to find this content?)
- Use **keyword-rich hashtags** (not just #fyp)
- **On-screen text** is indexed — your text overlays count as SEO content
- **Voiceover/speech** is transcribed and indexed — say your keywords out loud
- Check TikTok search bar for **autocomplete suggestions** to find popular search terms

### Instagram SEO
- **Alt text** — manually add descriptive alt text to every post (Settings → Advanced → Alt Text). Include keywords naturally. IG uses this for Explore page recommendations.
- **Caption keywords** — IG's search indexes caption text. Include relevant keywords naturally.
- **Profile name** — include your primary keyword (e.g., "Jack | Social Media Marketing")
- **Username** — if possible, include your niche keyword

### YouTube SEO (Descriptions)
YouTube descriptions are the most important SEO field in social media.

**Description template:**
```
[2-3 sentence summary with primary keyword in first sentence]

In this video, I cover:
- [Timestamp] [Topic with keyword]
- [Timestamp] [Topic with keyword]
- [Timestamp] [Topic with keyword]

🔗 Resources mentioned:
- [Link 1]
- [Link 2]

📱 Follow me:
- [Social links]

#keyword1 #keyword2 #keyword3
```

- **First 2-3 sentences** are most important (shown in search results, indexed heavily)
- Include **primary keyword in first sentence** of description
- **Timestamps/chapters** improve SEO and user experience
- Use **2-3 hashtags** in description (shows above title as clickable links)
- Fill out the full 5,000 characters — more text = more keywords = more search surface
- **Don't keyword stuff** — write naturally but strategically include search terms
