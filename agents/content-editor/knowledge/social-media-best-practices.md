# Social Media Best Practices by Platform (2025-2026)

## Instagram

### Content Dimensions & Specs
- **Feed Post (Square):** 1080×1080px (1:1)
- **Feed Post (Portrait):** 1080×1350px (4:5) — takes up more screen real estate, preferred
- **Feed Post (Landscape):** 1080×566px (1.91:1)
- **Reels:** 1080×1920px (9:16), up to 90 seconds
- **Stories:** 1080×1920px (9:16), 15 seconds per slide
- **Carousel:** Up to 20 slides, 1080×1080 or 1080×1350 (keep consistent within carousel)
- **Profile Photo:** 320×320px (displays as circle)
- **Max File Size:** 250MB for video, 30MB for images

### Algorithm Signals (2025-2026)
- **Sends/Shares** are the #1 ranking signal (content shared via DM)
- **Saves** remain a strong signal (bookmark-worthy content)
- **Watch time** and **replay rate** for Reels
- **Comments** weighted higher than likes
- **Follows from content** signal high quality
- **Original content** prioritized over reposts (Instagram's originality ranking)
- Reels shown to non-followers based on interest signals; feed posts primarily to followers

### Best Practices
- Post 4-7 Reels per week for maximum growth
- Use 3-5 hashtags (Instagram's own recommendation; fewer but more relevant)
- Carousel posts get highest save rates — use for educational content
- Stories: use polls, questions, quizzes for engagement; 3-7 stories/day
- Caption hook must be compelling — only first ~125 chars visible before "...more"
- Respond to comments within 1 hour for engagement boost
- Post when your audience is online (check Insights → Audience → Most Active Times)
- Collaborate feature for cross-audience reach
- Alt text on images for accessibility and SEO

### Optimal Posting Times (General)
- Best days: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- Best times: 9 AM–12 PM, 6–9 PM (audience local time)
- Worst: Sunday early morning, late night

## TikTok

### Content Dimensions & Specs
- **Video:** 1080×1920px (9:16), min 720×1280
- **Duration:** Up to 10 minutes; sweet spot 21-34 seconds for feed, 60-90s for storytelling
- **File Size:** 287.6MB (mobile), 500MB (desktop)
- **Formats:** MP4, MOV
- **Profile Photo:** 200×200px
- **Cover Image:** 1080×1920, auto-selected or custom

### Algorithm Signals
- **Completion rate** is the #1 signal (% of video watched)
- **Rewatch rate** — loops indicate high engagement
- **Shares** (especially to WhatsApp, Messages)
- **Comments** and comment depth (replies, conversations)
- **Follows from video** indicate interest match
- **Not interested** signals (skip, hide, report) hurt reach severely
- Content is served interest-graph first, social-graph second (unlike IG)

### Best Practices
- Hook in first 1-2 seconds — TikTok users decide faster than any other platform
- Use trending sounds — can 3-5x organic reach; check TikTok Creative Center
- Native, authentic content > polished production (the "made on TikTok" aesthetic)
- Text overlays are essential — 85%+ watch with sound off initially
- Post 1-3x per day for aggressive growth; minimum 3-5x per week
- Engage with comments — replies (especially video replies) boost distribution
- Stitch and Duet trending content for relevance signals
- Keep safe zones: top 150px (username/search), bottom 270px (caption/buttons), right 100px (engagement buttons)
- Hashtags: 3-5, mix of broad (#fyp is meaningless now) and niche topic tags
- Carousel posts (photo mode) are growing rapidly for educational content

### Optimal Posting Times
- Best days: Tuesday, Thursday, Friday
- Best times: 7-9 AM, 12-3 PM, 7-11 PM
- TikTok's algorithm is less time-sensitive than IG — good content surfaces regardless

## YouTube

### Content Dimensions & Specs

**Long-Form Video:**
- **Resolution:** 1920×1080 (1080p) minimum, 3840×2160 (4K) preferred
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9
- **Formats:** MP4 (H.264 codec, AAC audio)
- **Max File Size:** 256GB or 12 hours (whichever is less)
- **Thumbnail:** 1280×720px, <2MB, JPG/PNG/GIF

**YouTube Shorts:**
- **Resolution:** 1080×1920 (9:16)
- **Duration:** Up to 60 seconds (strict)
- **Uploaded vertically or tagged #Shorts**

### Algorithm Signals
**Long-form:**
- **Click-through rate (CTR)** — thumbnail + title combination
- **Average view duration (AVD)** — how long viewers stay
- **Session time** — does your video lead to more YouTube watching?
- **Engagement:** Likes, comments, shares, subscribers gained
- **Upload consistency** matters for channel authority

**Shorts:**
- **Swipe rate** (did they swipe away or keep watching?)
- **Completion/loop rate**
- **Engagement** (likes, comments, shares, subscribe)
- Shorts and long-form have SEPARATE recommendation systems

### Best Practices
- **Thumbnail is king:** High contrast, readable text (3-4 words max), expressive faces, curiosity gap
- **Title:** Front-load keyword, create curiosity, under 60 characters
- **First 30 seconds:** Hook viewers with what they'll learn/see; "pattern interrupt" editing
- **Chapters:** Add timestamps in description for automatic chapters (improves retention and SEO)
- **End screens:** Last 20 seconds — subscribe button + next video recommendation
- **Cards:** Link to related videos at relevant moments
- **Description:** First 2 lines contain keyword-rich summary (visible before "Show more"), then links, timestamps, social links
- **Tags:** Less important than before but still worth adding 5-10 relevant tags
- **Upload schedule:** Consistency matters — 1-2x/week for long-form, 3-5x/week for Shorts
- **Community tab:** Polls and updates increase subscriber engagement

### Optimal Posting Times
- Best days: Thursday, Friday, Saturday
- Best times: 2-4 PM (day before peak viewing) for long-form
- Shorts: 12-3 PM, 5-7 PM

## LinkedIn

### Content Dimensions & Specs
- **Image Post:** 1200×1200 (square) or 1200×627 (landscape)
- **Video:** 1920×1080 (landscape) or 1080×1920 (vertical/mobile), up to 10 min
- **Carousel/Document:** PDF upload, 1080×1080 or 1080×1350, up to 300 pages
- **Article Cover Image:** 1200×644
- **Profile Photo:** 400×400
- **Banner:** 1584×396

### Algorithm Signals
- **Dwell time** — how long users spend reading your post (most important)
- **Comments** (especially meaningful, multi-sentence comments)
- **Reposts with commentary** (shares)
- **Reactions** (weighted less than comments)
- **Profile strength** and creator mode signals
- **Content classification:** LinkedIn demotes overtly promotional content and "engagement bait"

### Best Practices
- Text posts and document/carousel posts get highest organic reach
- Hook in first 2 lines (visible before "...see more" on mobile, ~210 chars)
- Line breaks for readability — short paragraphs, 1-2 sentences each
- Storytelling format: personal experience → insight → takeaway → question
- Document carousels (PDF) are extremely high-performing for educational content
- Video is growing but still lower reach than text/document posts
- 1 post per day, 3-5 per week minimum for growth
- Engage heavily in first 60 minutes after posting (reply to every comment)
- No external links in posts (kills reach) — put links in first comment
- Hashtags: 0-3 max; LinkedIn's own data says they're minimally impactful
- Tag people only if they'll actually engage (don't spam-tag)

### Optimal Posting Times
- Best days: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- Best times: 7-8 AM, 12 PM, 5-6 PM (business hours, audience's timezone)
- Avoid weekends and holidays

## X / Twitter

### Content Dimensions & Specs
- **Post:** 280 characters max
- **Image:** 1600×900 (16:9) for single image, 1200×675 for link previews
- **Video:** 1920×1080 or 1080×1920, up to 2:20 (free) or longer (Premium)
- **Profile Photo:** 400×400
- **Header:** 1500×500
- **GIF:** Max 15MB

### Algorithm Signals
- **Replies** and conversation depth (the most weighted signal)
- **Retweets/Reposts** and Quote Tweets
- **Likes** (weighted less than engagement)
- **Bookmarks** (saves)
- **Time spent on tweet** (dwell time)
- **Profile authority** (follower count, verification, posting frequency)
- For You feed is algorithm-driven; Following feed is chronological

### Best Practices
- Concise, high-signal posts perform best — every word must earn its place
- Hot takes and strong opinions drive engagement (within brand safety)
- Thread format for long-form content: hook tweet → value tweets → CTA
- Post 3-5x per day for growth; minimum 1x/day
- Engage in conversations — reply to others in your niche
- Use visuals: images get 2x engagement, videos get 10x
- Polls for easy engagement (algorithmic boost)
- Hashtags: 1-2 max, only if genuinely relevant (overuse looks spammy)
- Space/audio rooms for thought leadership (if on Premium)

### Optimal Posting Times
- Best days: Monday–Friday (primarily a weekday platform)
- Best times: 8-10 AM, 12-1 PM, 5-6 PM
- Breaking news/trend-jacking has no "optimal time" — speed wins

## Facebook

### Content Dimensions & Specs
- **Feed Post Image:** 1200×630 (landscape) or 1080×1080 (square)
- **Video:** 1280×720 minimum, up to 240 minutes
- **Reels:** 1080×1920 (9:16), up to 90 seconds
- **Stories:** 1080×1920
- **Cover Photo:** 820×312 (desktop), 640×360 (mobile)
- **Profile Photo:** 170×170 (displays as circle)
- **Event Cover:** 1200×628
- **Group Cover:** 1640×856

### Algorithm Signals
- **Meaningful social interactions** — comments, shares, reactions between people who know each other
- **Content from friends and family** prioritized over Pages (organic page reach is ~2-5%)
- **Video completion rate** and watch time
- **Groups** content gets higher visibility than Page content
- **Reels** are being heavily pushed in 2025 (Meta competing with TikTok)
- **Link posts** get lowest organic reach (Facebook wants to keep users on-platform)

### Best Practices
- Facebook Reels are the highest-reach format currently — repurpose TikTok/IG Reels
- Native video (uploaded directly) >>> linked YouTube videos
- Groups strategy: build community, post natively in groups for organic reach
- Questions and polls drive comments (which drive reach)
- Facebook is a pay-to-play platform for businesses — budget for boosted posts and ads
- Events for local businesses get strong organic distribution
- Post 1x/day on Page; 2-3x/day in Groups
- User-generated content and employee advocacy posts outperform branded content
- Respond to all comments and messages (affects Page ranking and responsiveness badge)

### Optimal Posting Times
- Best days: Wednesday, Thursday, Friday
- Best times: 9-11 AM, 1-3 PM
- Weekend mornings for lifestyle/entertainment brands

## Cross-Platform Strategy Notes

### Content Repurposing Hierarchy
1. Create for TikTok/Reels first (highest organic reach potential)
2. Cross-post to Instagram Reels (remove TikTok watermark)
3. Repurpose to YouTube Shorts (re-edit if needed, different audience behavior)
4. Extract key insights for LinkedIn text/carousel posts
5. Create Twitter/X thread from longer content
6. Post native video to Facebook Reels

### Universal Principles
- **Consistency > virality** — regular posting builds algorithmic trust
- **Engagement begets engagement** — respond to every comment in the first hour
- **Quality > quantity** — one great post beats five mediocre ones
- **Platform-native > cross-posted** — adapt content to each platform's culture
- **Test and iterate** — use analytics to double down on what works
- **Accessibility matters** — captions, alt text, contrast, readable fonts
- **Sound-off first** — design for silent viewing, reward with sound-on
