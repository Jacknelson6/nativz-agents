# Platform Ad Specifications Reference (2025-2026)

## Meta Ads (Facebook & Instagram)

### Image Ad Specs
| Placement | Resolution | Aspect Ratio | Max File Size | Format |
|-----------|-----------|-------------|---------------|--------|
| Feed (FB/IG) | 1080×1080 | 1:1 | 30MB | JPG, PNG |
| Feed (Portrait) | 1080×1350 | 4:5 | 30MB | JPG, PNG |
| Stories/Reels | 1080×1920 | 9:16 | 30MB | JPG, PNG |
| Right Column | 1200×628 | 1.91:1 | 30MB | JPG, PNG |
| Marketplace | 1080×1080 | 1:1 | 30MB | JPG, PNG |
| Audience Network | 1200×628 | 1.91:1 | 30MB | JPG, PNG |

### Video Ad Specs
| Placement | Resolution | Aspect Ratio | Duration | Max Size | Format |
|-----------|-----------|-------------|----------|----------|--------|
| Feed | 1080×1080 or 1080×1350 | 1:1 or 4:5 | 1s–241min | 4GB | MP4, MOV |
| Stories/Reels | 1080×1920 | 9:16 | 1s–60s (Stories), 1s–90s (Reels) | 4GB | MP4, MOV |
| In-Stream | 1280×720 min | 16:9 | 5s–10min | 4GB | MP4, MOV |

### Carousel Ad Specs
- 2-10 cards per carousel
- Image: 1080×1080 (1:1) recommended
- Video: 1080×1080, up to 240 min per card
- Headlines: 40 chars recommended
- Description: 125 chars primary text recommended (longer allowed)

### Text Limits
| Field | Recommended | Maximum |
|-------|------------|---------|
| Primary Text | 125 chars | 63,206 chars |
| Headline | 40 chars | 255 chars |
| Description | 30 chars | 255 chars |
| Link Description | 30 chars | 30 chars |

### Campaign Objectives
- **Awareness:** Brand awareness, reach
- **Traffic:** Link clicks, landing page views
- **Engagement:** Post engagement, page likes, event responses
- **Leads:** Instant forms, Messenger, conversions
- **App Promotion:** App installs, app events
- **Sales:** Conversions, catalog sales, Advantage+ Shopping

### Targeting Options
- **Demographics:** Age (13-65+), gender, languages, location (country/state/city/zip/radius)
- **Interests:** Thousands of interest categories from user behavior
- **Behaviors:** Purchase behavior, device usage, travel, digital activities
- **Custom Audiences:** Website visitors, customer lists, app activity, engagement audiences
- **Lookalike Audiences:** 1-10% based on custom audience source
- **Advantage+ Audience:** AI-powered broad targeting with audience suggestions
- **Detailed Targeting Expansion:** Allow Meta to expand beyond selected interests

### Bidding Strategies
- **Lowest Cost (default):** Maximize results at any cost
- **Cost Cap:** Average CPA target
- **Bid Cap:** Maximum bid per auction
- **Minimum ROAS:** Target return on ad spend
- **Highest Value:** Maximize purchase value

## Google Ads

### Search Ad Specs
**Responsive Search Ads (RSA):**
- Headlines: Up to 15 (min 3), 30 chars each
- Descriptions: Up to 4 (min 2), 90 chars each
- Display URL paths: 2 paths, 15 chars each
- Final URL: Required

### Display Ad Specs (Responsive Display)
- **Landscape Image:** 1200×628 (min 600×314), max 5MB
- **Square Image:** 1200×1200 (min 300×300), max 5MB
- **Logo:** 1200×1200 (square), 1200×300 (landscape), max 5MB
- **Headlines:** Up to 5, 30 chars each
- **Long Headline:** 1, 90 chars
- **Descriptions:** Up to 5, 90 chars each
- **Business Name:** 25 chars

### Video Ad Specs (YouTube)
| Format | Aspect Ratio | Duration | Skippable |
|--------|-------------|----------|-----------|
| Skippable In-Stream | 16:9 or 9:16 | No max (12s min) | After 5s |
| Non-Skippable In-Stream | 16:9 | 15s or 20s | No |
| Bumper | 16:9 | 6s max | No |
| In-Feed Video | 16:9 | No max | N/A |
| Shorts | 9:16 | Up to 60s | After 5s |

### Shopping Ad Specs
- Product images: Min 100×100 (non-apparel), 250×250 (apparel)
- White background recommended
- No watermarks, logos, or promotional text on images
- Product title: Up to 150 chars (70 chars visible)
- Product description: Up to 5,000 chars

### Performance Max (PMax) Assets
- Text: 3-5 headlines (30 chars), 1 long headline (90 chars), 2-5 descriptions (90 chars)
- Images: 3+ landscape (1.91:1), 3+ square (1:1), 1+ portrait (4:5)
- Videos: 1+ video (horizontal, vertical, square) — Google auto-generates if not provided
- Logos: 1+ square, 1+ landscape
- Final URL, business name, sitelinks, callouts

### Targeting Options
- **Keywords:** Broad, phrase, exact match
- **Audiences:** In-market, affinity, custom segments, remarketing, customer match, similar audiences
- **Demographics:** Age, gender, household income, parental status
- **Location:** Country, region, city, radius, location groups
- **Device:** Desktop, mobile, tablet, TV
- **Ad Schedule:** Day of week, time of day

### Bidding Strategies
- **Maximize Clicks:** Automated for traffic
- **Maximize Conversions:** Automated for conversion volume
- **Target CPA:** Automated, target cost per acquisition
- **Target ROAS:** Automated, target return on ad spend
- **Maximize Conversion Value:** Automated for revenue
- **Manual CPC / Enhanced CPC:** Manual control with optional smart adjustments

### Spark Ads
- Boost organic TikTok posts as ads (from brand account or creator)
- Uses the original post — engagement accrues to the organic post
- Requires authorization code from creator (valid for 7, 30, 60, or 365 days)
- Higher CTR and engagement than standard in-feed ads on average

### Image Ad Specs (Carousel)
- 2-35 images per carousel
- Resolution: 1200×628 or 720×1280
- Format: JPG, PNG
- Max 100KB per image

### Text Limits
| Field | Characters |
|-------|-----------|
| Ad Description | 1-100 chars (12-80 recommended) |
| Display Name | Brand/app name, 2-40 chars |
| CTA | Select from predefined options |

### Campaign Objectives
- **Awareness:** Reach
- **Consideration:** Traffic, video views, community interaction
- **Conversion:** Website conversions, app install, lead generation, product sales (catalog)

### Targeting Options
- **Demographics:** Age (13-55+), gender, languages, location
- **Interests:** 15 interest categories with sub-categories
- **Behaviors:** Video interaction (liked, commented, shared), creator interaction, hashtag interaction
- **Device:** OS, OS version, device model, connection type, carrier
- **Custom Audiences:** Customer file, website traffic, app activity, engagement, lead gen, Shop activity
- **Lookalike Audiences:** Narrow/balanced/broad based on custom audience source
- **Smart Targeting:** TikTok's algorithm-based auto-targeting

### Bidding Strategies
- **Lowest Cost:** Maximize results within budget (no bid control)
- **Cost Cap:** Average CPA target
- **Bid Cap:** Maximum bid per result
- **Maximum Delivery:** Spend budget as fast as possible

## LinkedIn Ads

### Ad Specs by Format

**Single Image Ad:**
- Resolution: 1200×628 (1.91:1 landscape) recommended, 1080×1080 (1:1), 1080×1350 (4:5)
- File Size: Max 5MB
- Format: JPG, PNG, GIF (static)
- Intro Text: Up to 600 chars (150 recommended)
- Headline: 200 chars (70 recommended)
- Description: 300 chars

**Video Ad:**
- Resolution: Min 360p, recommended 1080p (1920×1080 or 1080×1920)
- Aspect Ratio: 16:9 (landscape), 1:1 (square), 9:16 (vertical)
- Duration: 3s–30min (15-30s recommended)
- File Size: 75KB–200MB
- Format: MP4
- Intro Text: 600 chars, Headline: 200 chars

**Carousel Ad:**
- 2-10 cards
- Image: 1080×1080 (1:1)
- File Size: 10MB per card
- Headline per card: 45 chars max

**Document Ad:**
- Upload PDF, DOC, DOCX, PPT, PPTX
- 300 pages max, 100MB max
- Great for lead gen (gate behind form)

**Message Ad (Sponsored InMail):**
- Subject line: 60 chars
- Body: 1,500 chars
- CTA button text: 20 chars
- Banner image: 300×250 (optional)

**Lead Gen Forms:**
- Up to 12 fields
- Pre-filled from LinkedIn profile data
- Custom questions available
- Thank-you message: 300 chars

### Campaign Objectives
- **Awareness:** Brand awareness
- **Consideration:** Website visits, engagement, video views
- **Conversion:** Lead generation, website conversions, job applicants

### Targeting Options (LinkedIn's Strongest Differentiator)
- **Company:** Name, industry, size (1-10, 11-50, 51-200... 10,001+), followers, growth rate, revenue
- **Job:** Title, function, seniority (Entry, Senior, Manager, Director, VP, C-Suite, Owner)
- **Education:** Degree, field of study, school name
- **Demographics:** Age, gender, location (country, state, city, metro area)
- **Interests & Traits:** Member interests, member traits, groups
- **Experience:** Years of experience, skills
- **Matched Audiences:** Website retargeting, contact list upload, company list upload, lookalike audiences, event attendees

### Bidding Strategies
- **Maximum Delivery:** Automated, maximize results within budget
- **Target Cost:** Automated with CPA target
- **Manual Bidding:** Set maximum CPC, CPM, or CPV

### Minimum Budgets
- Daily budget minimum: $10/day
- Lifetime budget minimum: varies by campaign duration
- CPC typically ranges $5-15 (higher than other platforms due to B2B targeting precision)
